# RepairEquipment

RepairEquipment is a system designed to manage the full workflow of an electronic equipment repair business. It allows you to register customers and equipment, create service orders, record diagnostics and observations, assign technicians, track repair status changes, and keep a complete history of each repair.

## Key Features

- Customer registration and management
- Equipment inventory (PCs, laptops, printers, etc.)
- Service orders with full lifecycle tracking
- Service statuses:
  - Received
  - Under Diagnosis
  - Under Repair
  - Ready
  - Delivered
- Repair history per equipment
- Technician management
- Technical observations and diagnostics
- Control of repair dates and times
- Role-based access control:
  - Administrator
  - Technician
  - Reception

## System Modules
